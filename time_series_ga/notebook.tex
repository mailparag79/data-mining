    \section{General explanation}\label{general-explanation}

Dickey-Fuller Test: This is one of the statistical tests for checking
stationarity. Here the null hypothesis is that the TS is non-stationary.
The test results comprise of a Test Statistic and some Critical Values
for difference confidence levels. If the `Test Statistic' is less than
the `Critical Value', we can reject the null hypothesis and say that the
series is stationary.
